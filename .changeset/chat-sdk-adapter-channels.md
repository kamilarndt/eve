The integrations catalog gains 17 Chat SDK adapter channels: the 4 official adapters for surfaces without a first-class eve channel (Google Chat, WhatsApp, X, Messenger) and 13 vendor-official adapters (Liveblocks, Resend, Sendblue, Zernio, Beeper Matrix, AgentPhone, Lark/Feishu, Velt, Kapso, Novu, Linq, Photon, Dial), each tagged Official or Vendor official with a `chatSdkChannel` quick start. The `eve channels add` picker is unchanged.
